Core platform updates

This About page reflects the current PixNest direction and the updates already added across the site.

Creator profiles

Creators have profiles with a cover image, profile picture, follower counts, following counts, post counts, badges, and creator identity that connects their posts across the platform.

Premium membership

Some posts can sit behind a premium wall. Premium members unlock those posts and may also display premium status on their account when membership is active.

Verification system

Users can submit a verification request. Once approved by admin, their account can show verified status and a blue check mark beside the creator name.

Admin approval flow

Creator uploads can be set to require admin approval, especially for creators who reach advanced platform milestones and move into a more controlled creator workflow.

Professional dashboard

The professional dashboard is reserved for stronger creator accounts. It can unlock automatically after reaching follower and view targets, or through admin approval after a submitted request.

Structured collections

Photos are grouped into categories, featured collections, trending selections, reposts, favourites, premium content, and creator-based experiences across the platform.

Frequently asked questions

These answers line up with the current PixNest feature direction.

Can anyone join PixNest?

Yes. People can browse, create accounts, and start building a profile. Advanced features unlock as accounts grow or get approved.

Are all posts free?

No. Some posts can be marked as premium, which means only premium members can unlock them.

How do I get verified?

Users can submit a verification request through the account menu. Once approved by admin, verified status can appear on the account.

How do I unlock the professional dashboard?

Either meet the follower and view targets or submit a request for admin approval.

Do uploads always publish instantly?

Not always. Approved creators may have uploads routed through admin review depending on the platform flow you are applying.

What does PixNest focus on most?

Creator identity, beautiful visual discovery, premium access, and quality-controlled platform growth.
