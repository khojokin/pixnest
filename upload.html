Submission Notes

These quick notes help keep the PixNest gallery clean, usable, and legally safe.

Image Quality

Use clear, high-quality images with good lighting, sharp focus, and clean composition.

Correct Category

Choose the category that best matches the image so visitors can find it faster.

Strong Titles

Give each upload a short, meaningful title that reflects the visual content clearly.

Rights and Ownership

Only upload images you own or are fully allowed to use and publish.

Before You Submit

Keep these simple rules in mind before adding content to the collection.

Use Original Work

Do not upload copied, stolen, or unlicensed content from other websites or creators.

Keep It Relevant

Submit visuals that fit the gallery style, categories, and overall quality direction of PixNest.

Write Useful Details

Strong titles, descriptions, and tags make photos easier to search and easier to use.

Explore the gallery too.
