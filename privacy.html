Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how PixNest handles personal information for visitors, account holders, uploaders, buyers, and premium members using the website.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how PixNest collects, uses, stores, and shares personal information when you browse the website, create an account, upload content, contact us, purchase premium access, or buy licences for locked photos.

By using the website, you acknowledge that your information may be processed as described on this page. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use the parts of the website that require personal information.

Effective Date: March 21, 2026

Website: https://pixnest.uk

Applies to: visitors, account holders, uploaders, buyers, and premium members

2. Information We May Collect

Depending on how you use PixNest, we may collect information such as:

  • Account details such as your name, username, email address, password sign-up data, and login activity
  • Profile details, preferences, membership status, saved items, and information you choose to add to your account
  • Contact form messages, support emails, complaints, and other communications sent to us
  • Photo uploads, captions, categories, tags, comments, and related metadata you submit through the website
  • Transaction details such as purchase history, premium plan status, licence type, order value, and payment confirmations
  • Technical and usage data such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages viewed, clicks, and referring pages
  • Cookie, analytics, and advertising-related data where those tools are active on the website

Payment card information is typically processed by third-party payment providers rather than stored directly by PixNest.

3. How We Use Information

We may use personal information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts and let users sign in securely
  • Provide access to unlocked photos, locked-photo purchases, and premium membership features
  • Process payments, verify purchases, and keep records of licences granted through the website
  • Host, display, review, organise, and moderate uploaded content
  • Respond to support questions, legal notices, takedown requests, or other enquiries
  • Improve the performance, security, design, and reliability of the website
  • Detect fraud, abuse, unauthorised access, chargebacks, or misuse of the platform
  • Send service-related emails such as account, purchase, login, renewal, and policy notices
  • Send marketing emails only where we are allowed to do so

4. Lawful Bases for Processing

Where UK data protection law applies, PixNest generally relies on one or more of these lawful bases:

  • Contract: where processing is needed to provide your account, premium access, purchases, or other services you requested
  • Legitimate interests: where processing is reasonably necessary to run, secure, improve, or defend the website and our business
  • Consent: where you have chosen to receive marketing, accept optional cookies, or otherwise given permission
  • Legal obligation: where we need to keep records, respond to lawful requests, or comply with legal duties

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

PixNest may use cookies, local storage, pixels, and similar technologies to keep the website working, remember settings, maintain sign-in sessions, understand traffic, and support analytics or advertising tools.

Some cookies may be essential for account login, payment flow, or security. Others may be optional, such as analytics or advertising cookies. You can usually control cookies through your browser settings, and where required by law we may ask for your consent before using non-essential cookies.

6. Analytics, Advertising, and Third-Party Services

PixNest may use third-party services such as website hosting providers, database and authentication services, payment processors, email delivery tools, analytics providers, embedded content providers, and advertising partners.

Those providers may receive or process certain information on our behalf according to their own policies and, where applicable, our agreements with them. If advertisements are displayed on the website, advertising partners may use cookies or similar technologies subject to the choices made on the site where required.

7. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information as a customer list. We may share information only where reasonably necessary, including:

  • With service providers that help us host, secure, analyse, email, authenticate, or process payments for the website
  • With professional advisers, insurers, auditors, or payment and fraud-prevention partners where needed
  • Where required to comply with law, enforce our Terms, investigate abuse, or protect rights, safety, or property
  • As part of a business sale, merger, investment, or restructuring involving the website or its assets

8. Uploads, Public Content, and User Responsibility

If you upload photos, profile content, or other material to PixNest, some of that information may be visible to other users or visitors, depending on how the website is designed. Please do not upload personal information you do not want to make available.

If you upload content containing identifiable people or other personal data, you are responsible for ensuring you have the rights, notices, permissions, or other lawful basis needed for that upload and any licence you apply to it.

9. Data Retention and Security

We keep personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including account management, transaction records, legal compliance, fraud prevention, dispute handling, and backup recovery.

PixNest uses reasonable technical and organisational measures to help protect personal information from unauthorised access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. However, no website, database, or online transmission can be guaranteed to be fully secure.

10. International Transfers and External Links

Some of our service providers may store or process information outside the UK. Where that happens, we may rely on appropriate safeguards permitted by law.

PixNest may also link to third-party websites, payment pages, social platforms, or external services. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those external services, and you should review their own policies before using them.

11. Your Rights

Depending on where you are located and the law that applies, you may have rights such as:

  • Requesting access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Requesting correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Requesting deletion of certain information
  • Requesting restriction of processing in some situations
  • Objecting to certain processing, including some marketing or legitimate-interest processing
  • Requesting a copy of certain information in a portable format
  • Withdrawing consent where processing depends on consent
  • Making a complaint to the UK Information Commissioner's Office or another relevant regulator

We may need to verify your identity before completing some requests.

12. Changes to This Policy

PixNest may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the website, the services offered, applicable law, or our data practices.

When we make material changes, we may update the effective date above and take reasonable steps to notify users where appropriate.
